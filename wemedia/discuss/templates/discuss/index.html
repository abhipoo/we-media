Recommendations

There is an inherent bias in every form of media currently available to masses. With the increasing use of AI in recommender systems, this bias seems to be growing unconstrained.

We-media attempts to show the user ‘what is not’ alongside ‘what is’. Counter views and context form the backdrop of all articles on the platform.

Technology is the supplementary arm of We-media. Topics / concepts / ideas are added to the graph by bots crawling the internet.

An ambitious goal for the platform and contributors is to identify and scrutinize the world views going around.
